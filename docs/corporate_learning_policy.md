## Certification Cost Reimbursement

### Eligibility

- Employee must have completed at least 90 days of employment.
- The certification must appear on the approved certification catalog maintained by TEAM-ENABLEMENT.
- Prior manager approval is required before scheduling an exam.

### Reimbursement Rules

| Item | Coverage |
|------|----------|
| Exam registration fee | 100% covered (first attempt) |
| Exam retake fee | 50% covered (second attempt only; third attempts are self-funded) |
| Official study materials | Up to $250 USD per certification track per fiscal year |
| Third-party practice exams | Up to $100 USD per certification track |

### Process

1. Employee submits a Certification Intent Form via PORTAL-LEARN.
2. Manager reviews and approves within 5 business days.
3. Upon passing, employee uploads proof of certification.
4. Finance processes reimbursement within 15 business days.

## Focus Hours Policy

### Entitlements

| Role Category | Weekly Focus Hours Allowance |
|---------------|------------------------------|
| Individual Contributors (Engineering) | 4 hours per week |
| Individual Contributors (Non-Engineering) | 2 hours per week |
| People Managers | 2 hours per week |
| Senior Leadership (Director+) | 1 hour per week |

### Guidelines

- Focus Hours should be blocked on the employee's calendar as recurring events labeled "Focus: Learning".
- Managers must not schedule meetings during declared Focus Hours except in P1/P2 incidents.
- Focus Hours do not roll over week to week.
- Individual blocks must be at least 30 minutes.
- Employees are expected to log Focus Hour activities in PORTAL-LEARN.

## Manager Responsibilities

1. **Quarterly Planning:** Identify at least one certification goal per team member.
2. **Monthly Check-Ins:** Discuss learning progress during 1:1 meetings.
3. **Dashboard Monitoring:** Review Team Certification Dashboard bi-weekly.
4. **Escalation:** If a team member is blocked, escalate to TEAM-ENABLEMENT within 10 business days.
