## Key Findings

1. **20-Hour Threshold:** Employees exceeding 20 meeting hours/week show pass rates dropping to 50% or below.
2. **Focus Hours Predict Success:** Learners utilizing ≥70% of Focus Hours passed at 79% vs. 44% for those under 50%.
3. **Premature Attempts:** 31% of failures came from learners with practice scores below 70%.
4. **Optimal Profile:** 12–16 meeting hours, 3.5–4.5 study hours, ≥75% Focus Hour utilization, practice score ≥82% → 81% pass rate.

---

## Recommendations

- Managers should bring active learners below 18 meeting hours/week during certification periods.
- Implement automated nudges when Focus Hour utilization drops below 60% for two consecutive weeks.
- Institute a soft gate requiring two practice scores ≥80% before exam scheduling approval.
